World of Warcraft: Wrath of the Lich King, also known as WotLK (which translates to "The Wrath of the Lich King"), is the second expansion of World of Warcraft, announced during BlizzCon in August 2007 in California. Most of the expansion's content takes place on the icy continent of Northrend and revolves around the plans of the Lich King, an entity that had previously appeared in The Frozen Throne.
